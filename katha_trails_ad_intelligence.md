## 2. Competitor Ad Breakdown

### 🏆 Closest Competitor: **Memory Lanes x Chor Bizarre**
- **Hook:** *"Every Sunday, the city reveals a different chapter. Join Jibin George through Old Delhi — from Digambar Jain Temple to Fatehpuri Masjid — then breakfast at Chor Bizarre."*
- **Format:** Single image + event post
- **CTA:** Learn More / See Details
- **What works:** Walk + meal bundle = high perceived value, easy yes
- **What's missing:** No story. No emotional hook. No specific history fact.

### 🏃 Thrill Blazers (Adventure + Heritage)
- **Hook:** Bharmour Heritage Walk bundled in a 10-day adventure tour
- **Audience:** Delhi-based adventure travelers
- **What it signals:** Heritage is a *feature*, not their product. Your walk IS the product.

### 📍 urbanaut.app
- **Hook:** *"Weekend plans sorted! From rooftop experiences to hidden culinary gems — discover what Delhi has brewing this week."*
- **Format:** App-driven discovery / aggregator
- **What it signals:** Curation and "hidden gems" language resonates. Borrow this.

### 🎨 Sensory Storytelling Brands (e.g., Meethi by WOK in the Clouds)
- **Hook:** *"A walk through festive aromas, bites of freshly made Gujiya… Holi isn't just celebrated, it's felt."*
- **What it signals:** **Sensory language sells experiences.** Not logistics — emotions.

## 3. The Gap Katha Trails Must Own

> [!NOTE]

## 4. Your Facebook Ad Strategy — Getting First Registrations

### 🎯 Target Audience Setup

**Audience 1 — Foreigners / Expats in Delhi**
- Location: Delhi NCR + 40km radius
- Language: English
- Interests: Lonely Planet, National Geographic, UNESCO, Rick Steves, History Channel, BBC History
- Behaviors: Frequent international travelers, Recently moved to a new location

**Audience 2 — Indian History Enthusiasts**
- Location: Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bangalore (weekend travelers)
- Age: 28–55
- Interests: INTACH, ASI (Archaeological Survey of India), Indian History, Mughal history, Old Delhi
- Behaviors: Online event bookings, Cultural event pages

**Audience 3 — Retargeting (once your website is live)**
- Website visitors who didn't register
- Instagram profile engagers
- Video viewers (50%+ watch time)

### 🎨 Creative Direction (Visuals)

| Format | What to Show | Why It Works |
|---|---|---|
| **Single Image** | Close-up of a storyteller speaking to a small captivated group at a heritage site | Human connection > monument shots |
| **Carousel** | Slide 1: Mystery question → Slide 2: Story reveal → Slide 3: The walk → Slide 4: Register | Builds curiosity before the ask |
| **Reels/Video** | 30-second: A dimly lit bylane, ambient sounds, then a storyteller's voice saying *"This street has a secret…"* | Most engaging for cold audiences |
| **Testimonial** | First walkers' reaction videos (Day 1 priority) | Social proof converts doubters |

> [!WARNING]
> **Avoid these creative mistakes** competitors are making:
> - Showing empty monuments (no people = no emotion)
> - Using stock photography (kills authenticity)
> - Leading with price or logistics
> - Overloading copy with itinerary bullet points

---

### 📣 CTA Strategy

For a **new brand with zero social proof**, use scarcity + low-friction CTAs:

- ✅ *"Reserve My Spot — Only 12 per walk"*
- ✅ *"Join the First 50 Walkers"* (founding member energy)
- ✅ *"Book Free / Pay What You Feel"* (for walk #1 to drive first registrations)
- ❌ Avoid "Learn More" — passive, low intent
- ❌ Avoid price-first messaging before you have reviews

## 5. 30-Day Launch Campaign Plan

| Week | Goal | Activity |
|---|---|---|
| **Week 1** | Build awareness | Run 2 video/reel ads targeting cold audiences. No hard sell. Storytelling content only. Budget: ₹500/day |
| **Week 2** | Warm up + first walk | Add "Reserve My Spot" CTA. Retarget video viewers. Announce Walk #1 date. Budget: ₹800/day |
| **Week 3** | Social proof | Film Walk #1. Edit quick testimonial clips. Launch testimonial carousel ad. Budget: ₹1,000/day |
| **Week 4** | Scale what works | Kill underperforming ads. Double budget on best-performing creative. Start Lookalike audience from registrants. Budget: ₹1,500/day |

**Total 30-day budget estimate: ₹25,000–₹35,000** (approx. $300–$420 USD)
